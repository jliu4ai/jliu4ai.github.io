About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student supervised by Efstratios Gavves and Jan-Jakob Sonke at the University of Amsterdam. I am also a member of VISLAB and ELLIS .

My research aims to develop human-centered machines that augment human capabilities in perception, reasoning, and interaction with the world. To achieve this, I am focusing on the following topics:

• Efficient Learning: few-shot segmentation, domain adaptation, prompt learning

• Human-AI Interaction: interactive segmentation, multi-agent cooperation

• Foundation Models: multi-modal learning, vision-language models, healthcare application
